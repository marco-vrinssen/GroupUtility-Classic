# GroupUtility-Classic

**World of Warcraft Classic addon** for simplified group management with quick commands.

## Features

- **Quick Ready Check**: Initiate ready checks with a short command (requires leader/assistant permissions)
- **Role Poll**: Start role polls with a simple command (requires leader/assistant permissions)
- **Fast Group Leave**: Leave party/raid quickly
- **Auto-Mark Party**: Automatically marks tanks and healers with raid icons based on their assigned roles (requires players to have assigned roles in the group)

## Commands

### /rc
Initiates a ready check for your group.

### /rc+
Initiates a role poll for your group.

### /q
Leaves your current party or raid group.

### /mp
Automatically marks party members with raid icons:
- Tanks get marked with Blue Square (icon 6)
- Healers get marked with Yellow Star (icon 1)
